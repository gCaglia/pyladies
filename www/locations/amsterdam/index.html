PyLadies AMS

Welcome!

PyLadies Amsterdam is a social group for Python programmers of marginalized genders, such as but not limited to non-binary people, trans people, and women at every experience level from the Amsterdam Area. Our mission is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events and social gatherings. Please feel free to join us!
